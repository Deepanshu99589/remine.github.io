Remine India Private Limited is a Private incorporated on 06 December 2022. It is classified as Non-govt company and is registered at Registrar of Companies, Uttarakhand. About the company


Remine India Private Limited has a closed-loop recycling process that ensures that the lead from batteries is reused to create new batteries or other products, reducing the need for mining new lead. They also have processes in place to recycle other electronic waste, such as printed circuit boards.
